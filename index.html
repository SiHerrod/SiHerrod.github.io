ABOUT

Northern Turbo is a specialist engineering consultancy providing support and troubleshooting services, technical designs, solutions and expert engineering on power generation, electrical and control projects.

Our expertise includes:

  • GE Turbine Control Systems, Excitation, LCI Static Starters
  • Railway E&P
  • Diesel Generators
  • UPS Systems
  • MCCs, Switchgear, Control Panels
  • PLCs and Industrial Automation & Control
  • Protection Relays
  • Electrical Design


OUR PEOPLE

Simon Herrod

Simon has more than 15 years’ experience working on complex electrical, control and instrumentation projects in power generation and rail. With a first class BEng (Hons) degree in electronic and computer engineering from the University of York, a HNC with distinction in electrical and electronic engineering and a time served apprenticeship in engineering, Simon went on to graduate from Network Rail’s Graduate engineering programme and General Electric’s international Field Engineering programme in New York.

For several years he led the installation and commissioning of large power generation projects across Europe, Turkey and Russia. Back in the UK he built and led a switchgear and control panel manufacturing business for a railway electrical contractor and is now working as a senior power engineer where he is engineering solutions for power distribution systems.
